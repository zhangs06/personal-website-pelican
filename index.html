This is my personal website describing some of my work. I am passionate about teaching and open source software. My work with the community has been mostly around QGIS and OpenDataKit. I also maintain some directory websites with free resources for the geospatial industry in India.

I am currently located in Bangalore, India and work as a Tech Lead/Manager for the aerial imagery team at Google. My day job involves managing a large GIS operations team and also writing Python code for processing large amounts of satellite and aerial imagery.
